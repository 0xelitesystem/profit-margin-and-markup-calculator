# Profit Margin and Markup Calculator

Margin and markup describe the same profit against two different bases: margin divides profit by the selling price and can never reach 100 percent, while markup divides the same profit by cost and has no ceiling. This tool computes price, profit, margin, and markup from the cost plus any one of the other three.

**Live demo:** https://0xelitesystem.github.io/profit-margin-and-markup-calculator/

## What it does

Choose what you know besides cost: the selling price, the margin percent, or the markup percent. Enter the cost and that one figure. The tool computes the rest and shows price, profit per unit, margin, and markup together, so the relationship between margin and markup is visible on the same numbers.

A 60 percent margin is a 150 percent markup on the same cost and price. Confusing the two is a common way a price ends up far below what it was meant to be.
