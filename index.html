Margin divides profit by the selling price; it can never reach 100%. Markup divides the same profit by the cost; it has no ceiling. A 60% margin is a 150% markup on the same numbers. Confusing the two is how a price ends up far below what it was meant to be.
Not financial advice. A calculator for arithmetic, not pricing strategy.
